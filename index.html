Origin

The T-Botz Backstory

Deep inside the T-Crown Workshop — a sprawling, neon-lit facility run by the mysterious designer known only as Twizz — the first T-BOT came online. Clad in an orange clay-composite shell and powered by a pulsing blue energy core, it was built for one purpose: to create, support, and never stop.

Word spread fast. The T-Crown had a squad. T-BOT, the commanding engineer. Mini T-Bot, the nimble companion. SMALLZ, the hands-on builder. Three units — different sizes, different strengths — united by the same mission and the same glowing blue eyes.

Their fuel? Creator-Blocks — luminous yellow cubes stamped with the mantra "CREATE +EVERYDAY." Each block is packed with creative energy. The more they build, the more they earn. The more they earn, the more they grow. The T-Botz don't rest. They just build.

Fuel System

Creator Blocks

Creator-Blocks are the lifeblood of the T-Botz operation. Every block is a unit of creative energy — earned through output, charged through action, and spent to power growth. The T-Crown Workshop runs 24/7 sorting, restocking, and deploying these glowing cubes.

Yellow blocks are FULL — charged with active creative energy. Black blocks are EMPTY — drained and queued for recharge. The mission: keep the inventory full. Keep creating.
