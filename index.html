Origin File

From the T-Crown Workshop

Deep inside a neon-lit facility run by the designer known only as Twizz, the first T-BOT came online — an orange clay-composite shell wrapped around a pulsing blue energy core, built for one purpose: to create, support, and never stop.

Word spread fast. The T-Crown had a squad. T-BOT, the commanding engineer. Mini T-Bot, the nimble companion. SMALLZ, the hands-on builder. Three units, three sizes, one mission — and the same glowing blue eyes.

Their fuel? Creator-Blocks — luminous cubes stamped "CREATE +EVERYDAY." The more they build, the more they earn; the more they earn, the more they grow. The T-Botz don't rest. They just build.
